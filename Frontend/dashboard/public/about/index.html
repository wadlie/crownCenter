About CROWN Center

CROWN Center is a capstone project made in conjunction with Dr. James Purtilo, Ankita Raturi, Brian W. Davis, and the people at Beltsville Agricultural Research Center. The project aimed to update and unify the current CROWN data aggregation pipeline.

Special thanks to: Steven Mirsky, Chris Reberg-Horton, Aurelie Poncet, Sarah Seehaver, Greg Austic, Manuel Di Cerbo, and Fola Adeleke-Adedoyin for providing us with the tools and knowledge necessary to see this project to completion.
